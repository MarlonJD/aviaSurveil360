# Product Vision

## Vision

AVIASURVEIL 360 moves CAA surveillance from disconnected spreadsheets, e-mails and document folders into a single traceable oversight workflow.

## Product promise

- Inspectors know what to inspect and review.
- Auditees know exactly what the CAA needs from them.
- Managers see risk, overdue findings and workload in real time.
- Admins can manage templates and rules without changing code.

## Scope boundary

AVIASURVEIL is not a personnel licensing product. It should remain a separate oversight/surveillance product, while sharing platform services with AVIALICENSE where useful.

## Core objects

1. Organization
2. Audit / Inspection
3. Checklist
4. Finding / Observation
5. CAP
6. Evidence
7. Review Decision
8. Report
9. Notification
10. Audit Log

## Product north star

Every finding must clearly answer:

- What is the issue?
- Who owns the next action?
- What is the due date?
- What must happen to close it?
- What has already happened?
