# Rapor Kataloğu

## MVP raporları

### Audit Report

Hedef: Inspector, manager, auditee.

İçerik: scope, organization, inspectors, checklist summary, findings, observations, due dates, CAP requirements, evidence status, issue date.

### Open Findings Report

Hedef: Inspector, manager.

İçerik: finding number, organization, severity, due date, current owner, next action, status.

### Overdue Findings Report

Hedef: Manager.

İçerik: overdue findings, days overdue, severity, organization, inspector, auditee contact, escalation status.

### Organization Findings Report

Hedef: CAA ve auditee, visibility'e göre filtrelenmiş.

İçerik: organization findings, open/closed status, CAP status, evidence status, closure history.

## Visibility rule

Auditee report versiyonları internal notes, inspector performance, other organizations, internal risk calculation ve draft enforcement notes içermemeli.
