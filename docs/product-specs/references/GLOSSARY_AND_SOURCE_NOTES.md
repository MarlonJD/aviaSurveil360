# Glossary and Source Notes

## Glossary

- **Surveillance:** Continuing oversight activity performed by CAA after approvals/certificates are issued.
- **Audit:** Planned or ad hoc oversight activity to assess compliance.
- **Inspection:** A narrower or operational oversight activity.
- **Auditee:** Organization being audited or inspected.
- **Finding:** Formal non-compliance or deficiency record.
- **Observation:** Improvement opportunity or note that may not be formal non-compliance.
- **CAP:** Corrective Action Plan submitted by auditee.
- **Root Cause:** Why the issue happened.
- **Corrective Action:** What will fix the issue.
- **Preventive Action:** What will prevent recurrence.
- **Evidence:** Files/records proving implementation.
- **Due Date:** Deadline for response or completion.
- **Overdue:** Past due date.
- **Closure:** Formal CAA decision that finding is resolved.
- **Escalation:** Moving a finding to manager/enforcement attention.
