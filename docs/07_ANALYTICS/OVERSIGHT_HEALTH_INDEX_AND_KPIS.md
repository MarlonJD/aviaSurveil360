# Oversight Health Index and KPIs

## Purpose

Oversight Health Index is a management indicator, not a legal decision engine. It should help managers identify surveillance delay, exposure and workload issues.

## Suggested formula

| Component | Weight |
|---|---:|
| Plan Completion Rate | 20% |
| Overdue Finding Ratio | 25% |
| Critical Finding Exposure | 20% |
| CAP Effectiveness | 15% |
| Repeat Finding Rate | 10% |
| Inspector Workload Balance | 10% |

## Bands

- 90–100 Excellent
- 75–89 Healthy
- 60–74 Needs Attention
- 0–59 Critical

## Core KPIs

- Planned Audits
- Completed Audits
- Plan Completion Rate
- Open Findings
- Overdue Findings
- Critical Findings
- Average Closure Time
- CAP Submission Time
- CAP Acceptance Rate
- Evidence Acceptance Rate
- Repeat Finding Rate
- Inspector Workload
- Auditee Response Rate

## Dashboard rule

Show six decision cards first. Do not build full BI in MVP.

## Safety rule

The index must not automatically penalize, suspend or revoke any organization. It can recommend management attention or increased oversight.
