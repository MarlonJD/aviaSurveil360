# Demo Senaryosu — Operator Audit

## Senaryo

Airline XYZ için Operator Audit: Incomplete Crew Training Records.

## Aktörler

- CAA Manager
- CAA Inspector
- Airline XYZ Auditee User

## Data

- Audit: 2026 Operator Audit
- Checklist: Flight Operations Audit
- Question: Are crew training records complete and up to date?
- Finding: OPS-2026-001
- Severity: Level 2 Major
- Due date: 30 days

## Akış

1. Manager dashboard açar ve 2026 surveillance planını görür.
2. Airline XYZ için Operator Audit planlanmıştır.
3. Inspector bugünkü assigned audit'i açar.
4. Inspector Flight Operations checklist başlatır.
5. Inspector crew training records sorusunu Non-Compliant işaretler.
6. Sistem organization, audit ve checklist reference prefilled finding form açar.
7. Inspector OPS-2026-001 Finding issue eder.
8. Airline XYZ auditee giriş yapar ve My Findings görür.
9. Auditee CAP submit eder:
   - Root cause: Training records were maintained manually.
   - Corrective action: Migrate to digital training system.
   - Preventive action: Monthly training record audit.
   - Target completion date: 15 July 2026.
10. Auditee Training_Record_Updated.pdf yükler.
11. Inspector evidence'ı inceler ve kabul eder.
12. Finding kapanır.
13. Manager dashboard güncellenir.

## Demo mesajı

Sistem e-mail follow-up yerine izlenebilir CAA–auditee workflow sağladı.
