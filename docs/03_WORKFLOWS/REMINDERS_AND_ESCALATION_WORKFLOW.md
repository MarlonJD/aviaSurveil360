# Reminders and Escalation Workflow

## Purpose

Prevent overdue findings and keep management aware of risk.

## Steps

1. 30-day reminder
2. 15-day reminder
3. 7-day reminder
4. Due today
5. Overdue
6. Manager escalation
7. Optional enforcement escalation

## Rules

- Use Due Soon and Overdue language
- Level 1 critical notifies manager immediately
- Repeated findings can trigger attention
- Escalation is not automatic enforcement unless configured

## UX notes

- Show current owner, due date and next action at the top of the screen.
- Keep history in timeline/tab, not as primary content.
- Use primary buttons that match the next action.
